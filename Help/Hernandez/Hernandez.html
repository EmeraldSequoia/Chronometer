Hernandez

This watch is named for the tiny village in New Mexico famous for Ansel Adams' photograph "Moonrise, Hernandez".

The outer sun and moon icons show the azimuths of the respective bodies; The inner two show their altitudes on a log scale.

The upper left button advances to the next sunrise; the lower left button advances to the next sunset; the upper right button advances to the next moonrise; the lower right button advances to the next moonset.
