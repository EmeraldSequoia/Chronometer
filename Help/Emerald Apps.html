Geneva is just one of Emerald Sequoia's iOS apps. We also have:

  • Emerald Chronometer, which includes Geneva plus 14 other watches. These watches include a variety of interesting complications not found on Geneva, including alarms, timers, and planets displays.

  • Emerald Chronometer HD, a version of Emerald Chronometer for the iPad.

  • Emerald Time, a digital clock with NTP synchronization.

  • Emerald Timestamp, captures the precise times of events and saves a log.

  • Emerald Observatory, a beautiful astronomical clock exclusively for the iPad.

Please visit our website or search for "Emerald Sequoia" in the iTunes App Store.
