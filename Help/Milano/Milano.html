Milano has a perpetual calendar with three retrograde complications, hands that snap back to their starting positions without going all the way around.

The blue hand shows the current month. The yellow hand shows the date. The green hand shows the day of the week. You can see them jump by entering Set mode and dragging the hour hand.

The magenta hand indicates the fraction of the remaining power reserve.

The year is shown in a window on the otherwise empty back.
