When running on an iPad with iOS 4.2.1 installed, the buttons at the bottom of the display on Emerald Chronometer HD do not work.

We don't know exactly why; we are unable to reproduce it ourselves, because all of our devices are already at iOS 4.3 or later, and Apple doesn't allow devices to be downgraded. Normally the simulator would be useful for this purpose, but the simulator is running iOS 4.2 (not 4.2.1) and doesn't reproduce the problem.

So unfortunately, though our preference would be to find a workaround, the best we can offer customers running iOS 4.2.1 is to upgrade their iPads to the latest OS. You can do this by connecting your device to iTunes and following the prompts to upgrade.
